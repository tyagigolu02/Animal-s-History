About Steppe Eagle

Characteristic Value
Size Length up to 85 cm
Wingspan Up to 2.2 meters
Weight 3 to 4.5 kg
Color Brown plumage with light feathers on head and neck
Diet Varied diet including small mammals, birds, and carrion
Habitat Open grasslands, steppes, and semi-desert areas
Distribution Found in Europe, Asia, and Africa
Conservation Status Vulnerable
