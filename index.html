Why Save Wildlife?

Saving wildlife is crucial for maintaining ecosystems, preserving biodiversity, and supporting human well-being through ecosystem services, medicinal resources, and economic benefits. It also holds cultural, aesthetic, and ethical value, ensuring a healthy environment for future generations.
